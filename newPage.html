Welcome to the Sudbury, Suffolk Chamber of Commerce

Your gateway to leisure, tourism and business in the Sudbury area. Set in the heart of the Stour Valley, an area of outstanding natural beauty, the market town of Sudbury is an ideal centre for both business and pleasure trips to East Anglia. Sudbury is an ancient market town dating back to Saxon times.

For centuries the weaving and silk industry has prospered here and many great houses and churches have been built, giving the town a major historical legacy. Sudbury has been used for television locations, most significantly for BBC's Lovejoy.


The Chamber of Commerce

The Chamber of commerce is an active group that supports all businesses in the Sudbury area. We provide a range of benefits, including:

  • Networking
  • Lobbying for business
  • Monthly business briefings
  • Support and mentoring

Business and the economy

We run and support activities to support the local economy. Some examples are:
  • Sudbury in Bloom
  • Careers Fairs
  • Buy local
  • Silk Festival
  • Support for Gainsborough's House


Visit Sudbury

Sudbury is an excellent place to visit. It is surrounded by the attractive countryside so often painted by Constable and Gainsborough and by quintessentially English villages. There are many fine pubs, restaurants and coffee shops in the town.
