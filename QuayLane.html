Quay Lane

At the bottom of the Market Hill, beyond the library is Friars Street, which holds many attractive old buildings. 400 metres along Friars Street is Quay Lane. Quay Lane is a the centre of many leisure activities. It leads between the cricket club and tennis courts and bowling green to the Quay Theatre and the Granary and the scout's headquarters.

The Quay Theatre

The Quay Theatre is a very popular facility in a restored maltings on a cut on the River Stour. It offers a year-round programme of dance, music, drama, film and children's theatre. There is a bar, and frequent exhibitions of local painters.

The River Stour Trust

The River Stour Trust is based in The Granary, a restored 18th century building once owned by The River Stour Navigation Company. It now houses the Sudbury Riverside Exhibition and Museum. The building is available for private functions and there are regular events throughout the summer. Boat trips down the Stour start here and cream teas are available at summer weekends.
